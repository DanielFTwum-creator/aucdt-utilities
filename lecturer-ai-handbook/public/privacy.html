LecturerAI Privacy Policy

Document Ref: TUC-INC-2026-PRIVACY | Effective Date: 12 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LecturerAI ("the Application"), developed by the ICT Directorate of Techbridge University College (TUC) in Oyibi, Ghana, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with the Ghana Data Protection Act (GDPA, Act 843), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other global privacy laws.

1. Data Collection and Usage

To support academic planning and faculty training, the Application handles personal data in the following categories:

  • Authentication Information: Secured administrator password checks used to authenticate diagnostics access.
  • Diagnostic Logging: In compliance with university audit norms, the system logs system actions, including IP address, action categories, timestamps, and outcomes. These are saved to secure MariaDB tables to preserve logging traces.
  • User Briefcase Data: Active prompt configurations, template choices, and academic briefcase records are saved in your browser's local sandbox state (localStorage). This data is stored locally and is never transmitted to remote on-premise servers without your consent.

2. Third-Party Integrations and Processing

To produce academic handouts and planners, the Application integrates with the following services:

  • Google Gemini API: Text formatting and prompt engineering requests are passed to Google's server-side secure endpoints. No personally identifiable details are sent to Google, and the secure developer API key remains completely masked within the server environment.
  • jsPDF Handler: Handout compilation occurs entirely inside the browser's local sandbox memory.

3. Data Retention and Security

The Application enforces strict data isolation and lifecycle security:

  • On-Premise Logging: System diagnostic audit logs are persistent to meet regional educational board (GTEC) auditing expectations.
  • Local User Data: Faculty members retain full autonomy over local browser storage. You can flush stored lesson configurations and saved briefcase items at any time by clearing your browser cache or choosing Clear Stored Workspace inside the application dashboard.

4. Your Legal Rights

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), and the GDPR, users hold absolute rights to:

  • Request access to check their on-premise audit traces.
  • Object to data collection or restrict processing.
  • Request deletion of system diagnostic logging records.
  • Verify compliance and seek remediation.
