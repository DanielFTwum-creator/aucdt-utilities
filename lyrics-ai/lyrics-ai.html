Create in Three Simple Steps

Follow this straightforward process to create your own 3D AI-augmented lyric animations in moments.

1

Choose Your Visual Theme

Select from a list of curated preset themes like 'Cosmos' or 'Forest', or type a custom prompt to let AI generate a unique background for you.

2

Enter Your Lyrics

Paste or type your song lyrics into the text area. Each new line will be treated as a separate scene for the animation, giving you full control over the pacing.

3

Animate & Record

Click the "Animate" button. The app processes each line, generating a dynamic 3D animation that is automatically recorded and downloaded as a video file.
