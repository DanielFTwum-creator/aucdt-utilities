\section{Brand Overview}
AUCDT represents excellence in higher education, combining traditional academic values with modern innovation in design and technology. Our brand identity reflects professionalism, creativity, and academic distinction whilst maintaining accessibility and contemporary appeal. This guide ensures our brand is presented cohesively and powerfully across all platforms.

\section{Brand Protection}

\subsection{Logo Don'ts}
\begin{itemize}[label={\color{AUCDTBurgundy}\faTimesCircle}]
    \item Do not stretch, distort, or change the orientation of the logo.
    \item Do not place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast.
    \item Do not alter the logo colours or add unauthorised effects.
    \item Do not crop or disassemble the logo components.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Implementation Checklist}
\begin{itemize}[label={\color{AUCDTGold}\faSquare[regular]}]
    \item Logo correctly sized and positioned
    \item Primary colours accurately reproduced
    \item Poppins font family implemented
    \item Proper contrast ratios maintained
    \item Brand voice consistently applied
\end{itemize}
