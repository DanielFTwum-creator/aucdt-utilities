How to Submit a Lecturer Evaluation

Getting Started

Before You Begin

What LEMS is LEMS (Lecturer Evaluation & Management System) is the TUC platform for collecting confidential student feedback on teaching quality. Your evaluations help the college maintain and improve academic standards in line with GTEC requirements.

Before opening the evaluation form, make sure you have the following ready:

  • Your TUC institutional email (@techbridge.edu.gh) and WMS password
  • The name of the course you are evaluating (check your timetable or course registration)
  • The name of the lecturer who taught that course
  • Approximately 5–10 minutes — the form cannot be saved mid-way
One submission only You may submit once per lecturer + course combination. Once submitted, your evaluation cannot be changed. Take your time and be honest before clicking Submit.
Step 1

Logging In

  1. Open the LEMS portal

    Visit lems.techbridge.edu.gh in your browser. You will see the LEMS login page.

  2. Enter your TUC email and password

    Use your @techbridge.edu.gh institutional email. This is the same login you use for WMS and other TUC platforms.

  3. Click "Sign In"

    You will be redirected to the Student Evaluation form. If you see the Admin Dashboard instead, you have admin access — use the student portal link to submit an evaluation.

Tip If you have previously logged into WMS in the same browser session, LEMS may log you in automatically without asking for your password again.
Step 2

Selecting Your Programme & Course

  1. Select your Programme

    Choose your programme from the dropdown (e.g. "B.Tech Fashion Design Technology"). Only programmes offered at TUC are listed.

  2. Select the Course

    The course list updates to show only courses belonging to your chosen programme. Select the course you are evaluating.

  3. Confirm the Lecturer

    The lecturer(s) assigned to that course are shown. Confirm you are evaluating the correct person before proceeding.

Recommendation Question

After the 10 criteria you will answer one additional question:

"Would you recommend this lecturer to other students?"

Select Yes, Neutral, or No.

Written Comment (Optional)

A free-text comment box lets you elaborate on your ratings. This is optional but encouraged — specific, constructive comments are more useful to the lecturer and management than ratings alone.

Writing a helpful comment Be specific. Instead of "Good lecturer," try "The lecturer always provided real-world examples that made the theoretical content easier to understand." Instead of "Bad," try "I would have appreciated more timely feedback on assignments."
Step 4

Submitting Your Evaluation

  1. Review your ratings

    Scroll through the form and check that every criterion has a rating selected. The form will not submit with missing ratings.

  2. Click "Submit Evaluation"

    The button is at the bottom of the form. Once clicked, the submission is final.

  3. Confirmation screen

    A confirmation message will appear thanking you for your submission. This confirms that your evaluation was received.

Your identity is never stored LEMS records a one-way cryptographic hash of your submission to prevent duplicates — not your name, student ID or email address. No-one at TUC can identify who submitted which evaluation.
After Submission

What Happens Next

Your ratings are aggregated with those of other students and reviewed by the Head of Department and relevant academic staff at the end of each evaluation period. Aggregate (not individual) results inform:

  • Staff professional development plans
  • Course content and delivery reviews
  • GTEC accreditation reports
  • Academic board quality assurance discussions

Individual submissions are never shared with lecturers. Only anonymous aggregated scores and unattributed comments are made available.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

I already submitted for this lecturer. Can I change my response?

No. Once submitted, evaluations cannot be edited or deleted. The system records a one-way hash that prevents duplicate submissions from the same student for the same lecturer+course pairing.

My course is not listed. What should I do?

Contact the ICT office at ict@techbridge.edu.gh with your programme name, course code, and lecturer name. Do not evaluate a different course as a substitute.

Can my lecturer see my individual ratings?

No. Lecturers only see aggregated scores and unattributed comments. Your identity is never recorded — not your name, student ID, or email address.

I submitted but did not see a confirmation. Was it recorded?

If the page did not show a confirmation, try submitting again. If you receive a "duplicate submission" error (409), your first submission was already recorded successfully.

Can I evaluate more than one course?

Yes. After confirming one submission, return to the form and select a different course (and lecturer) to submit another evaluation. Each course+lecturer combination is treated independently.

I cannot log in. What should I do?

LEMS uses the same login as the WMS platform. If you cannot log in, reset your password through WMS or contact the ICT help desk.

Is filling in the comment box compulsory?

No. The written comment is optional. You may submit with only the 10 rated criteria and the recommendation question answered.
