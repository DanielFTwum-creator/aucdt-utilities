Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Techbridge University College (TUC) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle project data within the Techbridge AI Blueprint [TAB] system.

1. Data Collection

We collect project names, progress states, and diagrams purely for the purpose of application lifecycle management and collaborative sharing.

2. Sharing

When you use the "Share Diagrams" feature, a read-only snapshot of your project state is stored in our secure cloud node and made accessible via a unique URL. Anyone with the link can view the shared content.

3. Security

Data is stored using industry-standard encryption provided by Google Cloud Infrastructure. Administrative access is restricted to authorized ICT personnel.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your shared snapshots or account data at any time by contacting the TUC ICT department.
