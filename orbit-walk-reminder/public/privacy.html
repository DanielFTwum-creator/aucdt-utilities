Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 May 2026

1. Data Collection

Orbit Walk is a productivity tool designed for the Techbridge University College community. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) of our student or staff users.

2. Local Storage

The application uses Local Storage on your device to persist your UI theme preference and session walk counts. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Audio Permissions

The application requires access to play audio cues (dings). No microphone access is requested or used.

4. Audit Logging

Administrative logins are logged for security purposes, including timestamp, action taken, and IP address. This data is used strictly for internal TUC-ICT security monitoring.

5. Your Rights

As no personal data is collected, users have full control over their application state by clearing browser cache or uninstalling the mobile application.

6. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, contact the Techbridge University College ICT Department at ict@techbridge.edu.gh.
