Privacy Policy

LuxThumb Designer | Last Updated: 10 May 2026

1. Overview

LuxThumb Designer ("App," "We," or "Us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App. By accessing and using LuxThumb Designer, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

a) Design Data

When you create designs in the App, we collect the content you enter or upload, including:

  • Brand name, taglines, and descriptive text
  • Headline and subheadline text
  • Colour choices and design preferences
  • Uploaded images (logos, subject images)
  • Custom configurations and settings

Storage: This data is stored locally on your device in browser IndexedDB or app storage. We do not transmit your design data to our servers.

b) AI Generation Requests

When you request AI-powered design suggestions, we send your prompts to Google's Gemini API. This includes:

  • Your product/brand description
  • Design preferences and requirements
  • Requested image generation prompts

Storage: Google processes and may temporarily store these requests according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store them on our servers.

c) Audit Log Data

For security and accountability, we log actions you perform within the App:

  • Export operations (PNG, JPG, PDF, JSON)
  • AI generation requests and completions
  • Admin panel access and configuration changes
  • Theme and accessibility preference changes
  • Timestamps of actions

Storage: Audit logs are stored locally in your device's IndexedDB. You can export or clear them via the Admin Panel.

d) Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device model, operating system, OS version
  • App version and build number
  • General usage patterns (feature frequency, export formats)
  • Crash logs and error reports (for debugging)

Storage: This data is collected via Google Analytics and stored according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • AI Suggestions: To generate design prompts and images via Gemini API
  • Security & Compliance: To maintain audit logs, detect fraud, and prevent misuse
  • Performance Improvement: To identify bugs, improve user experience, and optimise features
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and feature popularity (aggregated, non-personally identifying)

4. Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage (Your Device):

  • Design data is stored in browser IndexedDB or app-local storage
  • Audit logs remain on your device unless you export or delete them
  • Clearing app data will permanently delete all local information

Cloud Storage (Google Services):

  • Gemini API requests are processed by Google; retention governed by Google's policies
  • Google Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies
  • We do not store your design data on our servers

No Central Database:

Unlike many cloud applications, LuxThumb Designer is designed for privacy-first local operation. We do not maintain a central database of user designs or audit logs. Your data remains under your control.

5. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini API to generate AI-powered design suggestions. When you request design ideas or image generation prompts, your input is sent to Google's servers.

Google's Responsibility: Google processes your requests according to their Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We recommend reviewing these documents.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track app usage and performance. This helps us identify bugs and improve the user experience.

Opt Out: You can opt out of analytics tracking in the App's Accessibility settings.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communication with cloud services is encrypted in transit
  • Local Encryption: Audit logs can be exported with password protection
  • API Key Management: Gemini API keys are secured and never exposed in client code
  • No Cleartext Storage: Sensitive data is not stored in plain text

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can access your design data at any time by exporting your configuration as JSON from the App
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App
  • Right to Portability: You can export your audit logs and configuration data for use with other applications
  • Right to Opt Out: You can disable analytics tracking and decline AI generation features
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can revoke permission for the App to access device features (camera, gallery, etc.)

8. Children's Privacy

LuxThumb Designer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will immediately delete such data and terminate the child's access to the App. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country. By using LuxThumb Designer, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside of your country of residence, which may provide a different level of data protection than your home country.
