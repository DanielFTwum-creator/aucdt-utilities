Privacy Policy

Document Ref: TUC-INC-2026-PRIVACY | Effective Date: May 22, 2026
Techbridge University College (TUC), located in Oyibi, Ghana, is committed to safeguarding the privacy and data integrity of all students, faculty members, and external learners utilising the Techbridge AI Blueprint [TAB] / "An Elephant on Parade" Digital Companion application.

This privacy charter has been structured to satisfy the rigorous statutory requirements of the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843), the European Union's GDPR, and the US CCPA.

1. Data Collection & Processing Scope

To support high-integrity pedagogical operations without compromising student identity, our platform employs a strict **No Personal Data Harvesting** principle:

  • No Account Sign-Ups required: General curriculum users do not need to register, submit name logs, or link external social profiles to access the drum simulator, techniques board, or storytelling chapters.
  • Simulated Administrative Credentials: Administrative logins are managed via localized passcode checks. No centralized server-side identities are harvested or tracked.
  • Client-Side Local Storage: Application settings, customization options (such as theme preference and audio volume levels), and local developer diagnostic lists are recorded locally inside the user's browser LocalStorage sandbox. This logging never leaves the user's host terminal container.

2. Web Audio Spectrum and Hardware Usage

  • Microphone Permissions: The system requests frame permission for the device microphone to enable visual analyzer sweeps or interactive pitch trackers. This feed is processed entirely in client-side volatile memory. Sound input is never recorded, translated, compiled, or stored on external cloud nodes.
  • Audio Synthesis: Sounds are programmatically rendered locally using client-side JavaScript Web Audio nodes. No user listening analytics are sent back to our servers.

3. Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843) Compliance

Under the provisions of the GDPA 2012, TUC operates as a responsible Data Controller. Users retain rights to:

  1. Access local metadata: You can inspect all values stored in LocalStorage via standard browser Web Developer tools.
  2. Request data erasure: Clicking "Reset Base Configurations" in the Admin Center automatically sweeps, clears, and purges all local logs and saved preferences instantly.
  3. Submit Inquiries: Concerns regarding campus data standards may be directed to our dedicated information privacy registry department.

4. Children's Data Protection Standards

As classroom training often involves young primary school students engaged with Steve Ferraris' "An Elephant on Parade" curriculum, the application has been engineered to enforce absolute safe boundaries. The systems are completely ad-free, lack external messaging hubs or chat elements, and lack any third-party marketing trackers (such as Facebook or Google Ads cookies).

5. Ingress Security & Infrastructure Safeguards

The web shell runs inside hardened Docker nodes hosted in our Plesk platform, routed behind Nginx reverse proxies with TLS 1.3 encryption. Diagnostic incident records are formatted under our TUC-INC-2026 registration guidelines, allowing safe ICT oversight with zero leakage of private user footprints.
