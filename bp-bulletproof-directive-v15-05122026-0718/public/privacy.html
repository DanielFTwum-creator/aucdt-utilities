Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Techbridge University College (TUC) ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Compliance Workflow Dashboard application. This Privacy Policy outlines our compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Ghana Data Protection Act (GDPA) regarding the collection, storage, and processing of personal data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We may collect personal identification information (Name, Email address, Institutional ID) when you register, log in, or use our services. This data is utilized exclusively to provide administrative access, track compliance milestones, and record audit logs required for institutional oversight.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust, industry-standard security measures, including data encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, to safeguard your personal data. Administrative actions are logged for security auditing purposes.

4. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA / GDPA)

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to the processing of your data.
  • Request data portability.
