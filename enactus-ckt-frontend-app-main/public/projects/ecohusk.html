Ecohusk - Biowood & Biochar from Rice Husks and Straw

Biowood

Converts rice husks into biodegradable plywood, ceiling panels, and packaging boards.

Biochar

Transforms rice straw into eco-friendly charcoal, reducing deforestation and CO₂ emissions.

Impact

25–30 jobs created, 2900 lives impacted, affordable materials for communities.

Future

Expansion into eco-products like brushes, cups, and pest-resistant boards.
