Polygen - Plastic-to-Fuel & Eco-Bricks

Problem

Ghana produces over 1.1 million tons of plastic waste annually; floods, pollution, and housing shortages persist.

Solution

Through pyrolysis, Polygen converts plastic waste into diesel-like fuel and eco-friendly construction bricks.

Impact

For every 10kg of plastic, we generate 7 liters of fuel + 10 bricks. Annual revenue projected at ₵240,000 while removing 50 tons of plastic from the environment.

SDGs

#7 Clean Energy, #11 Sustainable Cities, #13 Climate Action
