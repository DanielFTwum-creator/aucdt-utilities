AgriHub - AI-Powered Digital Platform for Farmers

Problem

Farmers face limited market access, poor inputs, and post-harvest losses.

Solution

AgriHub offers a holistic platform: AI planting guidance, weather updates, verified buyer marketplace, crop disease detection, and video consultations with agronomists.

Impact

Farmers like Lydia in Northern Ghana sell produce directly online; youth like Peter find jobs as AgriHub Agents bridging rural digital gaps.

Revenue

Transaction fees (1–2%), subscriptions, premium consulting, and ads.
