Cocofert - Organic Fertilizer from Coconut Husk

Problem

Ghana's farmlands face declining fertility from chemical fertilizers.

Solution

Cocofert, an organic fertilizer made from coconut husk, compost, and perlite, tested and confirmed by CKT-UTAS Biological Science Department.

Impact

Improves soil fertility, water retention, and crop yield while reducing costs by 20–30% compared to chemical fertilizers.

Market

50kg bag sold at ₵360 (vs ₵500 for inorganic alternatives).
