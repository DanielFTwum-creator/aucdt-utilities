Privacy Policy

Application: VortexType
Institution: Techbridge University College (TUC), Oyibi, Ghana
Compliance Schema: GDPR, CCPA, and Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843)
Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Techbridge University College ("TUC", "we", "us", or "our") operates the VortexType mobile and web applications. We respect your concerns about privacy and are committed to safeguarding user data.

1. Data We Collect

This application is designed to be privacy-respecting and offline-first. The types of information processed include:

  • Practice Statistics: Keys parsed, speeds in Words Per Minute (WPM), typing accuracy percentage records, and general points scores.
  • Progression Data: Active lesson indexes, levels unlocked, and virtual game progress statistics.
  • Technical Audit Logs (Administrators Only): User access attempts, action statuses, system event classifications, and system performance evaluations. This logs are maintained strictly inside the host infrastructure.

2. Storage and Data Retention

All client performance indicators, levels, and user settings are persisted **locally on your device** (via HTML5 browser LocalStorage). We do not transmit or synchronise personal student credentials to public cloud databases unless explicitly configured inside university student portals.

Audit logs records are retained temporarily for diagnostic purposes to secure learning networks and are recycled after ninety (90) days.

3. Compliance Policies (User Rights)

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act (Act 843) and European GDPR rules, users have specific guarantees:

  • Right to Erase (Right to be Forgotten): You can instantly purge any collected records and statistics by clicking "Reset Progress" in your Statistics panel or clearing your browser's private state cache.
  • Right to Access: You can inspect accumulated typing analytics and logs at any time directly through the interactive charts and metrics screens.
