Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22nd May 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Techbridge AI Blueprint [TAB] system collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843).

1. Controller Details

The data controller for the Techbridge AI Blueprint application is:

Techbridge University College (TUC)

2. Information We Collect

Our commitment is to absolute data minimalisation. The Techbridge AI Blueprint application operates as a standalone administrative and reading utility. Specifically:

  • No User Account Tracking: Users are not required to create accounts or authenticate to read general publication records.
  • Local State Retention: Reading completion tallies, text contrast filters, and preferred themes (Light, Dark, High-contrast) are saved completely inside your client device's browser (Local Storage). We have no access to this information.
  • Administrative Controls: Access logs, device IP coordinates, and simulated system execution parameters for administrative activities are kept strictly on local operational states and cleared on exit.

3. Data Storage and Transfer

All functional user data is processed locally on the client's device web browser. No telemetry is exported to databases outside the host node container mapped in Techbridge University College (TUC) local host Plesk environment in Oyibi, Ghana.

4. Your Institutional Rights

Under Act 843 of Ghana and the GDPR framework, students, system operators, and general users hold the following rights over their processed values:

  • Right to Erasure (Forget): You can immediately purge all application state parameters by executing the reset routines mapped under the web browser application section relative to our domain.
  • Right to Restriction: You hold full liberty to reject browser cookies and local storage state writes via standard browser configurations.

5. Updates to This SOP Policy

We may make periodic amendments to this policy in coordination with TUC Academic Information Systems rules. Alterations are live when modified on this URL.
