## Logline & Concept

### Intimacy at City Scale

A nostalgic garden intimacy — blonde woman in white tank, khaki pants, layered gold necklaces; Jamaican man in black tee, maroon pants, curly hair — from Labadi Beach Hotel garden explodes onto a colossal Times Square billboard, blending tropical serenity with urban chaos.

The core fracture: her soft backlight halo caught through the diamond lattice trellis becomes a lens flare on the LED wall. Orange marigolds at their feet become taxi bokeh. The bench becomes an anchor in a city that never stops moving.

We don't composite a couple *onto* a billboard. We let the garden *rupture* through it — shattered glass glow, double-exposure, light leaking at the edges of the TECHBRIDGE barrier. Twilight holds both worlds at equal exposure.

**Director intent:** Keep the couple untouchable. No hard sell. No looking at camera. They are not advertising. The city is advertising *them*.

## Visual Language

### Fractured Double-Exposure

ARRIFLEX 35 • PANAVISION PRIMO • TWILIGHT • SHALLOW DEPTH • GRAINY TEXTURED → HIGH-FIDELITY PHOTOREAL PIVOT

- **Light — Golden Backlight Halo:** Diamond lattice trellis as gobo. Warm 2800K rim through leaves, creating starburst that maps to billboard LED bloom. Keep skin 1 stop over.
- **Texture — Shattered Glass Glow:** Fractured refraction pass. Not a clean double-exposure — glass cracks catch Empire State silhouette, blue C sign, taxi trails. Light bleeds at fracture seams.
- **Palette — Tropical / Urban Clash:** Marigold orange + khaki sand + maroon vs sodium yellow taxis, TECHBRIDGE safety yellow, Times Square cyan. Twilight sky bridges both.
- **Depth — Shallow / Deep Dialectic:** Garden: f/1.4 Primo, subject isolated, orange marigolds melt. Times Square: deep f/8 plate, everything sharp, chaotic. Billboard is the hinge.
- **Grain — Illustrated → Photoreal:** V1-V2 start illustrated, grainy, textured memory. V3 hard pivot to 8K photoreal master. Audience feels memory become present.
- **Anchor — TECHBRIDGE Barrier:** Always keep yellow TECHBRIDGE barricade in lower third. Grounds fantasy. It says: this is really Times Square. No set. Real street.

## Camera & Lighting

### Twilight Science

Clean Plate LOG — reference grade.

**Camera system**
- Body: ARRIFLEX 435 / ALEXA 65 for plates
- Glass: Panavision Primo 24, 35, 50, 85mm
- Format: 8K LOG • 16:9 anamorphic
- Final: Rec.709 + P3 D65 master
- Shutter: 180° • 1/48 • 24fps

**Exposure**

Garden skin at +1 over. Billboard LED at 100 nits in LOG, let it bloom in Rec709. Keep Times Square ambient 2 stops under key to let billboard dominate. Shoot blue hour only: 19:15-19:55.

**Lighting diagram**
- Garden: Natural backlight through trellis + 2x Skypanel S60 bounce for soft fill. No hard source.
- Times Square: Practicals only. LED billboard is key. Add 1x tube for TECHBRIDGE edge.
- Effect: Haze + fractured glass element for glow. Keep air thick, twilight humid.

**Lensing note:** "Shallow depth on memory, deep focus on reality. The billboard is where shallow meets deep. That transition is the whole film." — Director, on set

**VFX workflow**

2. TRACK → 8-point on markers plate
3. DEPTH → Separate crowd/barrier vs sky
4. ALPHA → Luma key billboard screen
5. COMP → Double-exposure in linear
6. GRADE → Rec709 + grain pass (6%)
