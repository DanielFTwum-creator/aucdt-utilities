Investing in Ghana's Creative Future

A strategic proposal for supporting Techbridge University College (TUC) to foster national innovation and entrepreneurship.

Who is TUC?

Techbridge University College is a unique, GTEC-accredited institution at the forefront of creative and practical education in Ghana. Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of innovators, designers, and entrepreneurs who will drive the nation's economic development.

🎓

Specialised & Accredited

Offering targeted, industry-relevant programmes in design and technology, fully accredited by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission.

💡

Practice-Based Learning

Our curriculum emphasises hands-on skills and real-world application, ensuring graduates are not just knowledgeable, but also job-ready.

🚀

Fostering Entrepreneurs

We empower students to become job creators, embedding business and entrepreneurship skills across all disciplines.

Our Programmes: Forging Ghana's Innovators

💎

Jewellery Design Technology

Transforming precious minerals into high-value finished products, championing local craftsmanship and value addition.

💻

Digital Media & Communication Design

Equipping students with skills for the digital age, from graphic design and web development to digital marketing.

👗

Fashion Design Technology

Cultivating designers who can lead the "Made in Ghana" brand with innovative and culturally rich apparel.

📦

Product Design & Entrepreneurship

Developing innovators who can conceptualise, design, and market new products for local and global markets.

Aligning with Ghana's National Vision

TUC's core activities directly support key pillars of Ghana's national development strategy. Our focus on practical skills, value addition, and entrepreneurship creates a tangible impact on the economy and society.
