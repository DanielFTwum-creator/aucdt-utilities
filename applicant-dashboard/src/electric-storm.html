Warrior Profile

DJ CyStorm

Alias: Electric Storm Warrior

Origin:

Forged in the heart of a mystical tempest, chosen by ancient thunder deities, she embodies the untamed spirit of the storm. Her Nubian royal lineage is intertwined with elemental magic, making her a conduit for nature's raw electrical fury.

Alignment:

Protector of Realms / Force of Nature. She fights to maintain balance, punishing those who would disrupt the natural order or exploit the innocent.

Warrior's Creed:

"Ride the lightning, command the thunder. The storm is eternal, and so is my resolve!"
